About Charles

He's Not Playing
Doctor. He Is the
Real Thing.

Charles T. Folsom Jr., RN — known online as Nurse Charles — has spent 37+ years in emergency rooms across the country. Trauma centers, critical care units, emergency departments. He's seen what energy drinks do to a 22-year-old heart. He's watched fentanyl-laced vapes kill teenagers. He knows what's actually happening inside your body when social media says you're "just fine."

He started making short videos on TikTok about what he sees in the ER every day — and it blew up. His most viral video, about a 19-year-old who died from a fentanyl-laced vape cartridge, hit over 7 million views. That's not influencer luck. That's clinical authority meeting a world desperate for real answers.

He's an active ER nurse, a healthcare executive, a doctoral student in healthcare leadership, and an entrepreneur. IAMNURSE LLC is his company. Health equity and real science are his mission.
